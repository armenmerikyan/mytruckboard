Why Preference Center Matters in 2017

During a recent conversation with a leading Customer Experience Management business analyst, I found myself momentarily dumbstruck. She had just stated 2017 to be "the year of preference management," a monumental moment for customers and enterprises to change the conversation and pivot toward engaging consumers based on their desired method of communication. Not to my surprise, I believe she's absolutely right, an industry recognized marketing experience professional just repeated what I had in mind.

But is it really true?

The data makes it clear that the time is now for formulized preference management. Recent surveys from Oracle and likes of such companies have highlighted the importance of Customer Preference Management key to proper customer experience management.
